Ned

Ned is a well-proven, hard-hunting dog with bundles of drive and works his heart out all day long doing what he loves. He was a joy to train and is very biddable. He has passed these traits on through the many healthy litters he has produced.

Depending on the colour of the bitch, Ned will throw mainly reds and goldens but has also produced a variety of colours and litter sizes.
