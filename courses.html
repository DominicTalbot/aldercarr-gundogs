Training

Courses We Offer

At Aldercarr Gundogs, we offer a variety of training courses to suit all levels of experience and needs. Whether you are looking to train your dog for basic obedience or advanced field trials, we have the right course for you.

Basic Obedience Training

Our basic obedience training course covers essential commands such as sit, stay, come, and heel. Perfect for young dogs or those new to training.

Advanced Obedience Training

This course is designed for dogs that have mastered basic commands and are ready to learn more complex behaviors and off-leash control.

Field Trial Training

Prepare your dog for field trials with our specialized training program. We focus on skills such as retrieving, scent work, and steadiness to shot.

Puppy Training

Start your puppy off on the right paw with our puppy training course. We cover socialization, basic commands, and house training tips.

Behavioral Modification

If your dog has specific behavioral issues, our behavioral modification course can help address problems such as aggression, anxiety, and excessive barking.

One-on-One Training

For personalized attention, our one-on-one training sessions are tailored to meet your dog's unique needs and your specific training goals.

Join us at Aldercarr Gundogs and experience the difference professional training can make. Our experienced trainers are dedicated to helping you and your dog achieve success.


Group Classes

Our group classes provide a social and supportive environment for training. Learn alongside other dog owners and benefit from shared experiences.

Private Lessons

Private lessons offer individualized training plans and focused attention from our expert trainers. Ideal for addressing specific issues or achieving advanced goals.

Workshops and Seminars

Attend our workshops and seminars to learn from industry experts. Topics range from basic training techniques to advanced field trial preparation.
