About Us

I have been training dogs for 16 years specialising in gundogs and basic obedience for family pets.

We currently work a pack of dogs picking up on shoot days from large shooting estates to small family and farm shoots.

We train our dogs to do what they were historically bred to do.

Training our dogs for picking up and sitting on peg to working in the beating line or under the gun on a rough shoot.

We aim to produce and bring on healthy dogs true to their breed and job.

Training Courses We Offer

At Aldercarr Gundogs, we provide a range of dog training courses tailored to different levels and needs. Whether you're looking to improve basic obedience or refine advanced gundog skills, we have something for every dog and handler.

Basic Obedience

This course is designed for dog owners who want to improve their dog’s basic behavior, including heel work, recall, and lead manners. It’s perfect for dogs just starting out in their training.

Gundog Training

Nurture your dog’s natural instincts and train them for gundog work. This course focuses on improving control, retrieval, and teamwork in the field, ideal for those with a working dog.

Whistle Training

Whistle training helps improve the communication between you and your dog, ensuring better control in a variety of environments. This course is ideal for owners wanting to work on advanced commands.

Behavioral Adjustments

If your dog has behavioral challenges, such as pulling on the leash or excessive barking, this course will help you address these issues in a positive and effective manner.

One-on-One Sessions

These lessons are specifically tailored for you and your dog to achieve your end goal, weather that is for a well behaved family pet or a well trained shooting companion we will create a program specifically for you to achieve your goal.
