Our Services

At Aldercarr Gundogs, we offer expert gundog training, obedience lessons, and picking up services. Whether you're looking to improve your dog's recall or prepare for the field, Sam provides personalized training to meet your dog's needs.

Obedience Training

Training Courses We Offer

At Aldercarr Gundogs, we provide a range of dog training courses tailored to different levels and needs. Whether you're looking to improve basic obedience or refine advanced gundog skills, we have something for every dog and handler.

Basic Obedience

This course is designed for dog owners who want to improve their dog’s basic behavior, including heel work, recall, and leash manners. It’s perfect for dogs just starting out in their training.

Gundog Training

Develop your dog’s natural instincts and train them for gundog work. This course focuses on improving control, retrieval, and teamwork in the field, ideal for those with a working dog.

Whistle Training

Whistle training helps improve the communication between you and your dog, ensuring better control in a variety of environments. This course is ideal for owners wanting to work on advanced commands.

Advanced Recall

Build on your dog’s recall ability and strengthen the bond between you and your dog. This course is designed to improve your dog’s responsiveness, even in distracting environments.

Behavioral Adjustments

If your dog has behavioral challenges, such as pulling on the leash or excessive barking, this course will help you address these issues in a positive and effective manner.

One-on-One Sessions

For more personalized training, our one-on-one sessions allow for tailored advice and focused training, based on your dog’s specific needs and challenges.
